Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of braces in Raipur?

At Vedantam Dental Clinic, braces start from ₹15,000 for metal braces, ₹22,000 for ceramic braces, and ₹35,000 for clear aligners. These are among the best affordable braces prices in Raipur. Book a free consultation for your exact estimate.

How long does braces treatment take?

Treatment duration depends on the complexity of your case. On average, braces take 12–24 months. Clear aligners may work faster for mild to moderate cases — 6–18 months.

Are braces painful?

There is mild soreness for 2–3 days after each adjustment, which is normal. This reduces over time as your teeth adjust. Clear aligners generally cause even less discomfort.

Can adults get braces?

Absolutely. There is no age limit for braces. Many adults at Vedantam Dental Clinic choose ceramic braces or clear aligners for a discreet treatment experience.

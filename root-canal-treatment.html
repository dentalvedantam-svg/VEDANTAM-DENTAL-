When Do You Need Root Canal Treatment?

🔴

Severe toothache

Persistent or throbbing pain in a tooth, especially when biting or applying pressure.

🌡️

Sensitivity to heat/cold

Lingering sensitivity to hot or cold food and drinks even after the source is removed.

🦷

Darkened tooth

A tooth that has become dark or discoloured may indicate nerve damage inside.

🫧

Swelling or abscess

Swollen gums, pimple on gums, or facial swelling near a tooth often signals infection.

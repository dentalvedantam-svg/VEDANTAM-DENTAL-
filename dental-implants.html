The Implant Process — Step by Step

Free Consultation & X-Ray

Dr. Agarwal examines your jaw bone density and designs your personalised implant plan.

Implant Placement

The titanium implant post is placed into your jawbone under local anaesthesia — completely painless.

Healing Period

The implant fuses with your jawbone over 8–12 weeks (osseointegration). Temporary crown provided.

Crown Placement

Your permanent, custom-made crown is attached — natural looking and fully functional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are dental implants painful?

The procedure is done under local anaesthesia so you feel no pain during surgery. Post-procedure soreness for 2–3 days is normal and managed with standard pain relief prescribed by Dr. Agarwal.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper oral hygiene, dental implants can last a lifetime. The crown on top may need replacement after 10–15 years.

What is the cost of dental implants in Raipur?

At Vedantam Dental Clinic, implants start from ₹25,000 per implant including the crown. We offer the best affordable dental implant treatment in Raipur. Book a free consultation for your exact cost.

Am I a candidate for dental implants?

Most adults with good general health and adequate jaw bone are candidates. Dr. Agarwal will assess your suitability through X-rays during the free consultation.
